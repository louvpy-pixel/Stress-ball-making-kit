Make Your Own Stress Balls

Stretch, fill, tie, and squeeze. Create crunchy filler balls or soft slime and clay squishies with one colorful hands-on craft kit.

  • 15 empty skins
  • 12 fun fillers
  • 17.6 oz Clear Slime

★ Style 1 Crunchy Filler Bead Stress Ball

1

Prepare tools

Prepare the included flaring tool, empty ball skin, and cable ties.

2

Stretch the opening

Use the flaring tool to stretch the ball skin opening.

3

Add filler beads

Add your favorite filler beads into the ball skin.

4

Prepare the cable tie

Remove the tool, stretch the opening slightly, and prepare the cable tie.

5

Close loosely

Secure the opening loosely with the cable tie. Do not fully tighten yet; leave space for the syringe.

6

Remove air

Insert the syringe to remove air from the ball skin. Repeat until all air is removed.

7

Tighten fully

Fully tighten the cable tie to prevent air leakage. Reuse the syringe to remove air if needed.

8

Trim & play

Cut off the excess cable tie. Your crunchy stress ball is ready!

★ Style 2 Soft Slime / Clay Stress Ball

1

Mix slime or clay

Take clay or slime, add coloring, and mix evenly. Wear the included gloves to avoid mess.

2

Add fun fillings

Add glitter, beads, or other fillings to the clay or slime.

3

Fill the ball skin

Use the flaring tool to stretch the ball skin, then add the clay/slime mixture.

4

Place rubber band

Put the rubber band over the flaring tool.

5

Secure the opening

Stretch the rubber band open, then slide the ball skin opening over the band to secure it.

6

Wipe & enjoy

After sealing, pull out the flaring tool, wipe away excess clay from the opening, and enjoy.

OR

Use cable ties

You can use any sealing method you like, such as cable ties.

OR

Use the plug

We include a plug for optional sealing. Glue is not included and can be purchased separately if needed.

Tip: We include 10 resealable bags for convenient storage of leftover filler beads after opening the original packaging.
